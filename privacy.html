Privacy Policy for Fashionista

Last updated: 2025/12/28

This Privacy Policy describes how Fashionista ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our App's functionality:

1.1 Account Information

To enable cloud synchronization and personalized experiences, functionality may require you to create an account.

  • Sign in with Apple: We collect your name and email address as provided by Apple, subject to your privacy settings.
  • Email Sign Up: If you sign up via email, we collect your email address and password (securely hashed).

1.2 User Content (Images & Data)

  • Wardrobe Data: We collect images of clothing items you upload, along with associated metadata (category, color, material, etc.).
  • Usage History: We track your "wear history" to provide insights on your most worn items.
  • Processing: Images are processed locally and may be sent to Google Gemini API for AI-based classification and enhancement.
  • Storage: Data is stored locally on your device and synchronized to our secure cloud servers (Supabase) to allow access across multiple devices.

1.3 Subscription Data

  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We may collect anonymous App User IDs to track subscription status (Premium/Ultra) and purchase history. We do not process or store your financial information directly; all payments are handled by the Apple App Store.

1.4 Location Data

  • We request access to your device's precise location to provide weather-based outfit recommendations.
  • Purpose: To fetch local weather conditions (temperature, rain, UV index).
  • Processing: Coordinates are sent directly to WeatherAPI.com. We do not store your location history.

1.5 Guest Mode Data

  • If you use the App as a guest, your data is stored primarily on your device.
  • We may assign an anonymous identifier (guestUUID) to your installation to manage feature limits (e.g., upload counts).
  • Warning: If you delete the App while in Guest Mode, your data cannot be recovered.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To organize your wardrobe, create outfits, and sync your data across your devices.
  • Personalization: To suggest outfits based on your local weather and wear history.
  • AI Analysis: To automatically tag and classify your clothing using Artificial Intelligence.
  • Account Management: To manage your subscription and verify your identity.

3. Third-Party Services

We partner with third-party services that may have access to your data to perform specific tasks on our behalf. They are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Supabase (Backend-as-a-Service)
    • Used for authentication, database, and file storage.
    • Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Subscriptions)
    • Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.
    • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini (Generative AI)
    • Used for image analysis and background removal.
    • Google Privacy Policy
  • WeatherAPI.com
    • Used for real-time weather data.
    • WeatherAPI.com Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Cloud Sync: Your wardrobe data and images are securely stored in the cloud (Supabase) linked to your account.
  • Data Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
  • Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account directly within the App settings. Deleting your account will permanently remove your login credentials and all associated data (images, items, history) from our servers.

5. Your Rights

You have control over your information:

  • Access & Portability: You can view all your stored items within the App.
  • Correction: You can edit clothing details at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items or your entire account via the "Delete Account" option in the Profile settings.
  • Permissions: You can manage specific permissions (Location, Camera, Notifications) in your iOS device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

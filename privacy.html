Privacy Policy for Fashionista

Last updated: 2025/11/24

This Privacy Policy describes how Fashionista ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our App's functionality:

1.1 Location Data

We request access to your device's precise location to provide weather-based outfit recommendations.

  • Purpose: To fetch local weather conditions (temperature, rain, UV index) and suggest appropriate clothing from your wardrobe.
  • Processing: Your coordinates are sent directly to our weather data provider (WeatherAPI.com). We do not store your location history on our servers.

1.2 User Content (Images)

We collect images of clothing items that you upload or capture via the App.

  • Purpose: To organize your digital wardrobe and provide AI-powered classification and image enhancement.
  • Processing: Images are processed locally and sent to Google Gemini API for analysis (classifying clothing type, color, material) and enhancement (removing backgrounds).
  • Storage: Your images are stored locally on your device. We do not store your photos on external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the App (organizing wardrobe, creating outfits).
  • To generate personalized outfit recommendations based on local weather.
  • To automatically classify and tag your clothing items using Artificial Intelligence.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • WeatherAPI.com
    • We use this service to fetch weather data based on your location.
    • WeatherAPI.com Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini (Generative AI)
    • We use Google's Generative AI to analyze and enhance your clothing images.
    • Data sent to this API is subject to Google's Generative AI terms and privacy practices.
    • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: All your personal data, including wardrobe images and preferences, is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults, File System).
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not currently maintain a cloud backend or user accounts. If you delete the App, all your data will be permanently deleted from your device.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data on central servers, you have full control over your information directly on your device:

  • Access: You can view all your stored data within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items or the entire App to remove all data.
  • Permissions: You can revoke Location or Camera permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
